ABOUT US

Home to a 46-bay, multi-tier, floodlit driving range, powered by Toptracer Range technology. Complimented by a practice green and bunker, coffee shop and American Golf Store. There truly is something for everyone as we also boast two outdoor 18-hole dinosaur themed crazy golf courses.

TOPTRACER RANGE

A cutting-edge golf driving range with advanced technology and interactive games for players to improve their skills and compete with friends.

ADVENTURE GOLF

Miniature golf with themed obstacles and quirky designs, perfect for families and casual golfers seeking entertainment and fun outdoors.

GOLF LESSONS

Personalized instructional sessions led by qualified instructors to help golfers of all levels improve their technique and performance on the course.
